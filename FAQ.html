Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Information

What are your opening hours?
We are open Monday to Friday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday to Sunday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Where are you located?
Our address is 123 Coffee Lane, Coffeeville, CA 90210. We are located downtown, right next to the City Park.

Do you offer free Wi-Fi?
Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi to all our customers. Just ask our staff for the password.

Is there parking available?
Yes, we have a parking lot behind the shop with ample space. Street parking is also available nearby.

Menu and Ordering

What types of coffee do you serve?
We serve a variety of coffee drinks including espresso, lattes, cappuccinos, Americanos, and cold brew. We also have a selection of specialty coffees that change seasonally.

Do you have non-dairy milk options?
Yes, we offer almond milk, soy milk, oat milk, and coconut milk as alternatives to dairy milk.

Can I customize my drink?
Absolutely! You can customize your drink with different types of milk, syrups, and extra shots of espresso.

Do you have any gluten-free or vegan options?
Yes, we have a selection of gluten-free pastries and vegan options. Our menu is marked with symbols to indicate these choices.

Loyalty Program and Discounts

Do you have a loyalty program?
Yes, we have a loyalty program where you earn points for every purchase. Once you accumulate enough points, you can redeem them for free drinks or discounts.

Do you offer student or military discounts?
Yes, we offer a 10% discount to students and military personnel with a valid ID.

Events and Catering

Do you host events or private parties?
Yes, we can host small events and private parties. Please contact us for more information and to make a reservation.

Do you offer catering services?
Yes, we provide catering for events and meetings. You can choose from a variety of coffee and pastry options. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to place a catering order.

Payment and Services

What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay).

Do you have gift cards?
Yes, we offer gift cards in various denominations. They can be purchased in-store or online through our website.

Additional Information

Are pets allowed?
Pets are welcome in our outdoor seating area. Service animals are allowed inside.

How can I provide feedback about my experience?
We value your feedback! You can leave a review on our website, social media pages, or fill out a comment card in-store.

How can I apply for a job at your coffee shop?
We are always looking for passionate coffee lovers to join our team. You can apply by filling out the application form on our website or by dropping off your resume in-store.

Do you have merchandise for sale?
Yes, we have a selection of branded merchandise including mugs, t-shirts, and tote bags. These make great gifts for coffee lovers!
